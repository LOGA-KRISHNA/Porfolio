Nayakan (1987)

A gangster film starring Kamal Haasan as a petty thief who rises to become a powerful don.
It is considered one of the greatest Tamil films ever made.

Anbe Sivam (1996)

A romantic drama starring Kamal Haasan and Urmila Matondkar.
It tells the story of a rich man who falls in love with a poor woman.

Pariyerum Perumal (2018)
